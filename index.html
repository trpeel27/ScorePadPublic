Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

ScorePad stores your game information locally on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, your data may sync through your personal iCloud account using Apple CloudKit.

Do I need an account?

No. ScorePad does not require a separate account or sign-in.

Can I use ScorePad offline?

Yes. You can create and edit games offline. iCloud sync, if enabled, may update later when your device is online.

How do I report a bug?

Email support with the steps to reproduce the issue and screenshots if helpful.
